Tyrannosaurus Rex

  1. Dinosaurs
  2. Theropods

16 July 2019 - Tyrannosaurus is a genus of coelurosaurian theropod dinosaur. The species Tyrannosaurus rex (rex meaning "king" in Latin), often called T. rex or colloquially T-Rex, is one of the most well-represented of the large theropods.
